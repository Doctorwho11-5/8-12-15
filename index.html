Notes for 8-12-15





Complementary Angles - Complementary are angles that their angles have their sum add to 90 degrees.

Supplementary Angles - The sum of their angles measure 180 degrees.

Adjacent Angles - A pair of angles that are adjacent are a pair of angles that share the common ray.

Linear Pair  - Two adjacent angles are a linear pair if their uncommon sides are opposite rays.

Vertical Angles - Two angles are vertical if their sides form two pairs of opposite rays.
